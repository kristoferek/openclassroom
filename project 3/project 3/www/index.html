About festival

Welcome to the Brooklyn Outdoor Film Festival

We are happy to announce that this year, 5 th-8th of August, there will be four day long film show. Nevertheless you are young, senior, fully capable or handicup - this event is available for you, your familly and friends.

Traditionaly great directors and celebrities will join us during last day Gala. According to fully filled agenda there are going to be presented 18 movies of domestic and foreign production. The show will take place in our beloved park Brooklyn Park - Pier 1.

This year jury gathered a truly proffessional and recognizable team. They will work together to award movies in six cathegories.

To deliver best watching experience we provided 5 000 free tickets for visitor. Please use our online registration form to book your free ticket today!
